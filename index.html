About Aakash Hospitals

Healing Excellence
Rooted in Kurnool

Aakash Hospitals stands as Kurnool's premier super-specialty destination, combining world-class infrastructure with deeply compassionate care. Led by Dr. P. Srihari and a team of distinguished specialists, we serve our community with 20+ medical departments and state-of-the-art technology.

From advanced neuro surgery to comprehensive gynaecology, our multidisciplinary team ensures every patient receives the most precise, personalised treatment available in Andhra Pradesh.
